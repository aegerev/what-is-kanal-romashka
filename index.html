Here To Help Everyone.

Young children need a variety of academic and social-emotional skills to succeed inside and outside the classroom. That's why Kanal Romashka utilizes a "whole-child approach" to preparing children for life, offering media that supports key developmental areas — from communication, social and emotional development, and critical thinking skills to literacy and STEM (science, technology, engineering and math).
All of this is combined with our patented "No Hosts, No Ads" protocol. We're here to help, not to hinder kids' educational needs.
